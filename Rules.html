Mahjong Game Rules

Objective:

The objective of the game is to match all tile sets until all are removed from the board.

Rules:

  • Two tiles match if they are the same kind of tile and their ranks are equal.

  • A tile can be removed when either the left or the right side does not have a tile immediatley next to it.

  • A tile can be removed if there is not a tile on top of it.

  • Any two season tiles match as do any two flower tiles.
